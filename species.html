Sydney's Most Popular Species

  • Bream - Found in estuaries, likes prawns & worms. Light tackle preferred.
  • Flathead - Caught with soft plastics and pilchards.
  • Kingfish - Try live bait near harbor structures.
  • Luderick - Needs green weed as bait, best at rocky spots.
  • Murray Cod - Targeted in Upper Hawkesbury freshwater areas.
