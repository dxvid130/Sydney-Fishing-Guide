        <h2>NSW Size Limits & Safety Rules</h2>

        <p>Always check the latest NSW DPI rules: <a href="https://www.dpi.nsw.gov.au/fishing/recreational/fishing-rules-and-regs" target="_blank" style="color:#49d4ff;">NSW DPI Rules and Regulations</a></p>


        <h3>Sample Size Limits (as of 2024)</h3>

        <ul>

            <li><strong>Bream:</strong> Minimum 25cm</li>

            <li><strong>Flathead:</strong> 36–70cm for Dusky, 33cm for others</li>

            <li><strong>Australian Bass:</strong> 30cm (closed season May-August)</li>

            <li><strong>Bag limits apply. Check DPI for more info.</strong></li>

        </ul>


        <h3>Fishing Safety</h3>

        <ul>

            <li>Wear a lifejacket when rock fishing along the coast.</li>

            <li>Observe closed areas and seasons.</li>

            <li>Check weather and tides before heading out.</li>

            <li>Respect protected species. Return undersized or out-of-season fish carefully.</li>

        </ul>
