Beginner Tips & Guides

  • Learn basic rigs: Running sinker, paternoster, float.
  • Use correct knots (see video below!)
  • Pick the right bait and rod for your target fish.
  • Fish early or late for best results.
  • Observe others' techniques at public spots.
