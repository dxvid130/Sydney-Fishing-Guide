NSW Size Limits & Safety Rules

Fishing in NSW is regulated! Always check the latest rules at the official NSW DPI site.

  • Bream: 25cm minimum
  • Flathead: 36cm minimum
  • Kingfish: 65cm minimum
  • Murray Cod: 60cm minimum, must be released over 100cm

Safety Rules

  • Always wear a lifejacket when rock or boat fishing
  • Check weather/tide conditions before you go
  • Let someone know your plan
